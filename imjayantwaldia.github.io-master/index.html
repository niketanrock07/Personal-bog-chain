The Guardian - TechX Panel

I was really lucky to be invited to participate in a panel discussing teens and tech along with the future of the internet when I met Harry de Quetteville at Ada after impressing him with a presentation on Machine Learning he saw me give.

News Digital Academy

I was happy to find that I had been accepted to take part of the News Digital Academy during the summer for a two week period with News Corporation (The Times) where I was teamed up with 3 developer, 2 designers and 7 journalist to make a news app in 1 week.

News Digital Academy (2)

We created an app called 'Natter' which targetted tourist readers. The USP of the app was that it was video based and whenever a place or restaurant was mentioned in a video it will allow you to get more details about the place and get directions or in some cases reserve places at the restaurant mentioned through affiliate links.

User Testing with Ustwo (1)

We were extremely privileged to have the game development company Ustwo come in twice in 2 months for a whole day in order to help us learn how to effectively user test our prototype and final game solution with our target audience.

Deloitte IoT Project Day (1)

We had a 3 day IoT project/Hackathon run by Deloitte where our task was to develop an IoT solution for a school that would benefit and aid the teachers and students. This solution would then be demonstrated to industry professionals.

User Testing with Ustwo (2)

This is me, user testing my game with my target audience which is mainly primary school students from 6 to 10 years-old. My game was built to teach kids to code and understand how to use browser's developer tools to modify CSS and HTML on a webpage.

Deloitte IoT Project Day (2)

This was my team for the 3 day IoT challenge where I was the solutions developer and team lead. What we developed was a noise synthesiser which measures noise level in a room and notifies people to be quiet vocally or alerts a teacher if students are being too loud.
